Big Sur

Big Sur is a long stretch of coastline in California along Highway 1, located about 45 minutes from Monterey and Carmel. It's an amazing place to hike and relax, and it features many of the things we love so much about nature in California — redwoods, rolling hills, beaches, and rocky coastline. The surrounding areas of Carmel, Monterey, and Carmel Valley feature world class golfing, wineries, and an excellent aquarium.

We're excited to spend a weekend with you in this beautiful, wild place!

Lodging

Book early

Big Sur doesn't have much lodging and fills up very quickly (it can book up to a year in advance!). Try to book as early as possible. If you're booking a hotel in Big Sur that's not Big Sur Lodge (our room block), don't tell them you're coming for a wedding!

Get to the wedding

We will provide transportation between Carmel and the venue on Saturday. That means if you're staying anywhere in between (e.g., Big Sur Lodge, camping, airbnb in Notley's Landing or Carmel) there will be a shuttle! Just let us know where you're staying.

Rough it?

The amount of protected land in Big Sur means camping opportunities abound! Camping will be close to the venue and welcome dinner. Let us know if you're interested!

Treat yourself?

The venue is very close to the highly rated luxury resorts Ventana and Post Ranch Inn. They're probably pretty nice.

Coming alone?

Lodging while traveling alone can be tricky! We may have some available glamping tents on the property that we can offer for a nominal fee. Fill out this form if you're interested!
